Welcome to Sivasakthi Stone Quarry

We provide high-quality stones, statues, and stone processing services. From raw rock mining to custom stone sculptures, we are your trusted partner in stone craftsmanship.

At Sivasakthi Stone Quarry, we specialize in:

  • Natural Rock Mining – Sourcing high-quality raw stones from reliable quarries.
  • Stone Processing – Turning raw materials into refined, cut, and shaped stone blocks and slabs.
  • Custom Stone Statues – Designing and sculpting unique stone statues as per customer specifications.
  • Finished Stone Goods Export – Safely transporting heavy stone goods using industrial-grade vehicles and logistics.

Whether you're seeking traditional statues, temple stonework, or raw construction materials, we cater to a wide variety of residential, commercial, and spiritual projects.
